## Reddit post draft
Title: I built a prototype where your ChatGPT/Claude dating summary becomes an AI dating proxy. Would you trust this?

Post:
Dating apps feel low-signal: swiping, dead chats, and first dates that were obviously not a fit.

I built a prototype called ProxyDate. The idea is simple:
1. Ask ChatGPT/Claude for a dating-safe summary of your values, lifestyle, interests, intent, dealbreakers, communication style, and preferred first dates.
2. Paste that summary into ProxyDate.
3. It creates a private dating proxy.
4. Your proxy compares compatibility with another person’s proxy.
5. Both humans approve before any intro.

No ChatGPT account connection. No full history. Copy/paste only.

Would you use this to screen matches before texting/meeting, or does it feel too weird?
